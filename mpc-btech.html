BTECH - MPC

B Tech is a four-year undergraduate degree programme that typically covers the fundamental principles and applications of engineering and technology. The coursework for this degree is designed to prepare students for careers in various engineering fields, such as mechanical, electrical, or computer engineering. In some cases, students may also be able to specialize in a particular area of engineering, such as environmental or industrial engineering.

BTech Course Eligibility Criteria:

>Candidates must have passed the class 12 exam from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as core subjects.

>They must also have secured minimum aggregate marks of 60% in the above subjects combined.

>The regular mode of entry to BTech course is through entrance examination after Class 12.

>Those who possess three-year diploma course in engineering can enrol in BTech under Lateral-Entry Scheme, wherein candidates can enrol in BTech course in second year.
