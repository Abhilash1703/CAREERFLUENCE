B.Com(Bachelor Of Commerec)

BCom is a three-year UG degree course recognised by the Universities Grants Commission (UGC). Students can pursue B.Com programmes as full-time BCom, Distance BCom, or Online BCom. BCom courses are one of the most popular courses amongst the Class 12 students with the Commerce stream. According to a survey report, with nearly 9.33 lakh enrolment, BCom has been the third most desired course in India after Class 12.

B.com Courses:

Chartered Account, BCom Marketing, BCom Banking and Insurance, and BCom Finance, BCom Computer Applications, BCom Economics, BCom Business Administration, BCom Teaching, BCom Journalism.

Jobs:

Accountant(4-7LPA), Financial Analyst(3-7LPA), Tax Consultant(3-8LPA), Business Consultant(5-10LPA), Stock Broker(3-5LPA), Auditor(4-9LPA).

E-commerce Courses

E-commerce is a rapidly growing career option. A good number of jobs with different profiles are being created in this sector every year. Seeing the current scenario of the digital age, a career in the e-commerce industry is one of the best career options chosen by students for their future. E-Commerce can be conducted through various applications like email, online catalogues & shopping carts, Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) and other web services.

E-Commerce Courses:

Bachelor of CommerceE-Commerce Management

Job Profiles:

E-Business Consultant, Business Analyst, Supply Chain Manager, Project Manager, Database Adminstrator.
