Bachelor of Science (BSc)

The 3-year undergraduate BSc degree is recognised across India. BSc courses include research, calculation, and experimenting. Both Honours and General courses can be taken in BSc courses.

BSC - Computer Science

A BSc Computer Science is a 3-year UG course that focuses on the study of computer systems, software development, algorithms, data structures, and computer theory.

Scope Industries: Banks(Computers and Related Electronic Equipment Manufacturers), Consultancies(Financial Institutions), Government Agencies( Schools and Colleges), Software Development Companies(Security and Surveillance Companies).

Jobs: IT Architect(3-5LPA), Data Analyst(4-5LPA), Database Administrator(4-6LPA), Information Security Officer(3-5LPA).

BSC - Agriculture

A Bsc Agriculture course is a UG degree program that focuses on various aspects of agriculture and related fields. The BSc Agriculture course duration is 4 years.

Scope - Indusries: Pharmaceutical Industry(Research Centers), Chemical Firms(Public Relations), Actuaries(Academic Institutions)

Jobs: Land Geomatics Surveyor(3-6LPA), Soil Forestry Officer(2-8LPA), Nursery Manager(2.5-5LPA), Grafting expert(2.6-7LPA).

BSc - Biotechnology

A Bsc Biotechnology is a 3-year UG course that focuses on the application of biological principles and processes to technological advancements and innovations.

Scope Industries: Research firms(Chemical manufacturers), Hospitals(Educational institutes), Pharmaceuticals(Fertilizer manufacturers), Food production firms(Soil biology), Clinical research firms(Animal husbandry), Laboratories(Cosmetics).

Jobs: Bioinformists(3-6LPA), Epidemiologists(5-10LPA), Biotech Specialists(2.5-5LPA), Biomanufacturing Specialists(3-6LPA).

BSC Honours Courses:

BSC - Physics, BSC - Mathematics, BSC - Botany, BSC - Ecology, BSC - Envirinment Science

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

BBA full form is Bachelor of Business Administration. BBA courses are a 3-year UG degree course designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business and management principles. The curriculum of BBA courses aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in various roles within the business world.

Top Specializations:

BBA Finance, BBA in Banking and Insurance, BBA Human Resource, BBA Marketing, BBA Hotel Management, BBA Foreign Trade, BBA Communication and Media Management, BBA Hospital and Healthcare Management.

Bachelor of Arts (BA)

BA full form is Bachelor of Arts. The Bachelor of Arts course is one of the most demanding undergraduate programmes and can be completed in three years. BA can be pursued in various disciplines such as English, Hindi, History, Journalism, and Psychology. BA course helps develop a variety of skills such as general research, deep analysis, and communication.

Specialization Courses: Political Science , History , Economics , Philosophy , Archaeology , Communication Studies

Job Profiles: Content Writer(3-5LPA), Editor(4-6LPA), Journalist(3-5LPA), Clinical Social Worker(3-5LPA), Counsellor(2-5LPA),

B.Ed (Bachelor of Education) - Future Course

B.Ed course is designed as a professional course aimed at preparing individuals to teach at various levels, including upper primary or middle level (classes VI-VIII), secondary level (classes IX-X), and senior secondary level (classes XI-XII). The B.Ed Division offers this program for aspiring students who want to become school teachers as well as for Assistant Teachers. B.Ed. is commonly regarded as the initial formal training for educators, although its main emphasis is on preparing teachers for secondary school education

B.Ed Eligibility Criteria:

> Educational Qualification: Candidates need to have completed their graduation in any stream (that is, Arts, Science, or Commerce) to fulfill B Ed eligibility criteria. However, most popular B Ed colleges allow candidates to take part in their admission process for B Ed courses if they have graduated with at least 50-55% aggregate marks at the UG level

> Age Limit: For B Ed admissions in a majority of colleges, there is no age bar. However, the admission process of some B Ed colleges requires candidates to be a minimum of 19 years of age.

Job Profiles: School Teacher(2-5LPA), Curriculum Developer(3.5-7LPA), Private Educator(2-4LPA), Career Counsellor(4-6LPA), Education Administrator(6-9LPA)
