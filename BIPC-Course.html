MBBS(Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)

Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery. MBBS course duration is 5.5 years with 5 years of academic study and 6 months of internship. An MBBS course is an undergraduate medical degree awarded to students who have completed their training in the field of medicine and surgery. The MBBS course is recognized in many countries as the standard qualification for medical doctors and physicians.

MBBS Eligibility Criteria:

To qualify for MBBS course, students must attain satisfactory scores in the NEET UG exam. Additionally, NEET eligibility states that students have completed their 12th-grade education with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Biotechnology as compulsory subjects.

MBBS Specializations:

Ophthalmology : Ophthalmology is a medical surgical discipline concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of eye problems.

Orthopedics : Orthopaedic surgery, sometimes known as orthopaedics, is the discipline of surgery concerned with musculoskeletal problems.

Anaesthesiology : The medical speciality of anesthesiology, sometimes known as anaesthesiology or anaesthesia, is concerned with the whole perioperative treatment of patients before, during, and after surgery.

Dermatology : Dermatology is the branch of medicine that deals with skin problems.

General Medicine : General medicine treats and manages a wide range of long-term ailments, such as diabetes and respiratory disorders, as well as shorter-term health issues, such as skin conditions.

Psychiatry : Psychiatry is a medical speciality concerned with the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of mental illnesses.

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) : Otorhinolaryngology is a surgical specialisation of medicine that deals with the surgical and medicinal care of head and neck diseases.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology : Obstetrics and Gynaecology is the medical speciality that comprises both obstetrics and gynaecology.

Paediatrics : Paediatrics is the medical speciality concerned with the medical care of infants, children, adolescents, and young adults.

Job Profiles:

Junior Doctor(4.5LPA), General Physician(3.5-7.5LPA), Medical Researcher(5-12LPA), Medical Surgeon(6-15LPA), Paediatrician(6-13LPA), Medical Assistant(2-4LPA), Medical Officers(5-10LPA).

B.Pharm(Bachelor of Pharmacy)

B Pharmacy, also known as Bachelor of Pharmacy, is a 4-year UG course that focuses on pharmaceutical sciences. A B Pharmacy is an academic degree program in the field of Pharmacy which focuses on pharmaceutical sciences, drug discovery, drug delivery systems, pharmacology, pharmacokinetics, pharmaceutics, medicinal chemistry, and clinical pharmacy. The B Pharmacy course curriculum usually includes both theoretical knowledge and practical training in various aspects of Pharmacy practice.

Eligibility Criteria:

The eligibility criteria for a B Pharmacy course are very basic and easy to fulfil. However, it is important to note that though B Pharmacy eligibility criteria are general guidelines, the specific requirements may vary depending on the institution the candidates are applying for.It is advisable to check the official websites of the universities or institutions students are interested in for accurate and detailed B Pharmacy eligibility information.

Job Profile:

Pharmacist(2.6LPA), Drug Inspector(3.2LPA), Quality Control(2.8LPA), Clinical Researcher(4-7LPA), Hospital Pharmacist(2.8LPA)

Bachelor of Science in Agriculture

A BSc Agriculture course is a UG degree program that focuses on various aspects of agriculture and related fields. The BSc Agriculture course duration is 4 years. The BSc Agriculture course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of agricultural practices, technologies, and policies.BSc Agriculture course provides students with a thorough understanding of the numerous facets of Agriculture, including crop production, soil science, animal husbandry, and agricultural economics.

Job Profiles:

Land Geomatics Surveyor(4.5LPA), Soil Forestry Officer(3.8LPA), Soil Quality Officer(4.6LPA), Plant Breeder/Grafting expert(4.8LPA), Seed/Nursery Manager(3.8LPA)

BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology courses cover a range of scientific disciplines that involve the use of biological systems, organisms, or derivatives to develop or create new technologies. Biotechnology courses are designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, techniques, and applications of Biotechnology.

Future Scope Industries:

Research firms(Chemical manufacturers), Hospitals(Educational institutes), Pharmaceuticals(Fertilizer manufacturers), Food production firms(Soil biology), Clinical research firms(Animal husbandry), Laboratories(Cosmetics), Consultation agencies(Ecology)

Job Profiles:

Biotech Analyst(2-4LPA), Research Scientist(6-12LPA), Biochemist(4-8LPA), Epidemiologist(5-12LPA), Lecturer/Professor(2-4LPA).
